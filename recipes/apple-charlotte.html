Easy Apple Charlotte Recipe

Delicious Homemade Apple Charlotte

Apple Charlotte is a classic, easy-to-make dessert with a soft, fluffy texture and a rich apple flavor. Made with simple ingredients like eggs, sugar, flour, and fresh apples, this cake is perfect for any occasion. Its golden-brown crust and tender, juicy apple slices inside create a delightful balance of sweetness and tartness. A light dusting of powdered sugar on top adds the perfect finishing touch.

This traditional dessert is a favorite for cozy family gatherings or a sweet treat with tea or coffee. The aroma of baked apples and vanilla fills the kitchen, making it impossible to resist. Whether served warm or at room temperature, Apple Charlotte is a comforting, homemade delight that brings warmth and happiness with every bite.

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup (200g) sugar
  • 1 cup (130g) all-purpose flour
  • 3 medium apples (peeled and sliced)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Butter (for greasing the pan)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a baking pan with butter.
  2. Prepare the batter: In a large bowl, whisk eggs and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add dry ingredients: Sift in the flour, baking powder, and salt. Mix until smooth. Add vanilla extract if using.
  4. Prepare the apples: Peel, core, and slice the apples into thin pieces.
  5. Assemble the cake: Place the apple slices in the greased pan and pour the batter over them.
  6. Bake for 30-40 minutes or until golden brown and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool & Serve: Let the cake cool slightly before serving. Enjoy with tea or coffee!

Tip: You can sprinkle powdered sugar or cinnamon on top for extra flavor.
