Easy Chocolate Brownie Recipe

Rich & Fudgy Chocolate Brownies

Chocolate brownies are the ultimate indulgent dessert, combining a rich, fudgy texture with an irresistible deep cocoa flavor. Made with simple ingredients like butter, sugar, cocoa powder, and chocolate chips, these brownies are incredibly easy to prepare. Their slightly crisp top and gooey center create the perfect balance between chewy and soft, making them a favorite for any chocolate lover.

The warm aroma of freshly baked brownies fills the kitchen, making it hard to wait before taking a bite. Whether enjoyed on their own, dusted with powdered sugar, or paired with a scoop of vanilla ice cream, these homemade brownies bring comfort and sweetness to any moment. Perfect for sharing with family and friends, they’re a timeless treat that never disappoints.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter (melted)
  • 1 cup (200g) sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ cup (60g) all-purpose flour
  • ⅓ cup (40g) cocoa powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • ½ cup (90g) chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease a baking pan or line it with parchment paper.
  2. Mix wet ingredients: In a bowl, whisk melted butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla extract, whisk until smooth.
  3. Add dry ingredients: Sift in flour, cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder. Gently mix until combined.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips for extra richness (optional).
  5. Pour into the pan and spread evenly.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the center is slightly set (a toothpick should come out with a few moist crumbs).
  7. Cool & Serve: Let the brownies cool before cutting. Enjoy with ice cream or a dusting of powdered sugar!

Tip: For fudgier brownies, slightly underbake them and let them set while cooling.
